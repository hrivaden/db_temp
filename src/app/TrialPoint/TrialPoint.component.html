TrialPoint™ is a comprehensive Research Management System (RMS) crafted to streamline data collection and provide immediate access to essential research information for clinical trials worldwide. Developed by Databean’s expert team, TrialPoint™ maximizes research efficiency, enabling teams to manage more studies with fewer resources.

A Clinical Trial Management System Built Around Your Needs

TrialPoint™ offers an adaptable suite of tools designed to support every stage of clinical research. The EDC (Electronic Data Capture) module ensures seamless data management with easy setup and automated reminders for team efficiency. The ePRO (Electronic Patient-Reported Outcomes) module empowers patient engagement through secure, mobile-friendly data entry and automatic compliance scoring. The CSM (Clinical Supply Management) module enables precise tracking of investigational products, while the Device Accountability module ensures thorough, compliant oversight of medical devices. Together, these modules equip you with full control and insight into your trial operations from start to finish.
