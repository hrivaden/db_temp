Our Story

Bridging Innovation and Expertise

From academic roots to industry expertise, Databean's leadership team came together in 2007 with a shared vision: to fill the gap for comprehensive, affordable clinical trial solutions for medtech and biotech start-ups.

Our mission has always been to empower these innovators and accelerate their path to market success.

  • Therapeutic Areas

    Our team has a wealth of experience in designing and managing clinical trials across a diverse array of therapeutic areas, such as neurology, heart failure, diabetes, lung and liver diseases, and end-stage renal disease. By incorporating innovative health economics strategies, we strive to maximize trial impact and ensure research findings translate effectively into improved patient care.

    Our work bridges the gap between clinical investigation and practical healthcare solutions, addressing both chronic and acute medical needs.

  • Drugs and Biologics

    With deep expertise in clinical trial design and oversight, we play a vital role in advancing the development of drugs and biologics for challenging cardiovascular and systemic health conditions.

    Our experience includes conducting trials for heart failure, arrhythmias, ischemic heart disease, hypertension, and cardiogenic shock, as well as research in pain management and immune modulation. We are committed to ensuring that clinical studies are thorough and adhere to regulatory standards, paving the way for therapeutic progress.

  • Class III Medical Devices

    We bring specialized knowledge to the design and management of clinical trials for Class III medical devices, essential for advanced medical treatments. Our expertise covers devices for cardiovascular care, neuromodulation, pulmonary health, diabetes, renal support, drug delivery systems, and tissue regeneration/biologics.

    By upholding the highest quality standards in clinical trials, we support the development of safe, effective, and innovative medical devices that enhance patient care and contribute to the evolution of modern medicine.

Michael Parides, Ph.D
Biostatistician

Michael Parides is the Director of Biostatistics at the Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai, NY, and oversees trial design and data management for Databean. Michael earned his Ph.D in Biostatistics from Columbia University and has 25+ years of experience in the design of complex clinical studies. He has been the principal statistician on numerous landmark trials in stroke and cardiovascular disease and employs a pragmatic approach to trial design for novel technologies, believing strongly in Bayesian techniques. Michael has developed excellent working relationships with the NIH and the FDA, serving as a DSMB committee chair and panel advisor. Despite his solid “pocket protector” status, Michael has a fantastic sense of humor and a passion for new technologies.
